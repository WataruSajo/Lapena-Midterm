Batangas: Top Destinations

1. The Monte Maria Shrine

On top of our list is one of the newest tourist destinations in Batangas, The Monte Maria Shrine, which is a place dedicated to Mother Mary. The statue of the Virgin Mary is 98 meters tall—as tall as eight buses upright. This place is not only popular with pilgrims, but also to locals and tourists. Everyone is welcome here. The Shrine is easy to access because it is just 45 minutes away from Batangas City.

2. Fantasy World

This place should undoubtedly be on the list, especially if you love taking photos and exploring abandoned places. The Fantasy World isan abandoned amusement park located in Lemery, Batangas. Though it may feel quite creepy, Fantasy World is still popular with tourists. Every turn showcases a picturesque view, perfect for both Instagram-worthy shots and colorful subjects for photo collections.

3. Saint Padre Pio Parish and Shrine

The Padre Pio Shrine is a pilgrimage site and is one of the most famous destinations in Sto. Tomas , Batangas If reflection and meditation is a must for you to ground yourself when traveling and exploring places, visiting the Padre Pio Shrine is going to be bliss for you.
